Ling.craft is a digital project whose purpose is to collect data from colonial sources on indigenous languages of the territory that is currently Colombia. Its broad goal is that researchers and non-specialists can access to information from books, manuscripts, files, or websites in one place, along with historical and linguistic metadata.
